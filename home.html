About Us
The mission of the club is to expose students to challenges and new technologies in the field of electronics and thus to nurture and develop their skill set in this arena.
We believe in coming up with projects that are innovative and tackle real life problems. To achieve these objectives we conduct various lectures, workshops and mentorship programs to help students realize their projects.

Regular meetings and mentorship programs will definitely help students to achieve their goals. The emphasis is on experimenting and learning, no experience is necessary but eagerness to learn is essential.
